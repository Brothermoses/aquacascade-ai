Operating model

How AquaCascade works

A clear chain from national prioritization to utility imports, field confirmation, rehabilitation planning, and long-term asset renewal.

01

National Prioritization

A model trained on EPA data ranks water systems by the likelihood that unknown service lines are lead-rich. This is a system-level signal, not a per-pipe claim.

02

Utility Inventory

Utilities import their own private service-line records. Location and geometry stay explicit: the system never invents pipe routes.

03

Inspection Queue

Inspectors receive only inspection work, ordered by priority, and record LCRR-compliant evidence from any device.

04

Rehab Queue

Confirmed Lead and GRR records flow into replacement planning and create work orders for replacement crews.

05

Compliance Exports

Results roll into the register, SDWIS view, model-feedback file, and rehabilitation plan exports.

06

Post-Lead Renewal

After lead is removed, the same workflow flags aged non-lead assets for long-term renewal.

Role Separation

INSPECTION Inspectors

Only see unknown-line confirmation work.

REHAB / RENEWAL Replacement crews

Only see replacement and renewal work.

DISPATCH Dispatcher / Admin

See the operational command center, imports, maps, plans, and exports.
