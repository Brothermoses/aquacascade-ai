## The bottom line (verified)

- **Venture product (final):** calibrated unknown-line lead-triage model. Repeated-CV ROC-AUC **0.71** (95% CI 0.705–0.716), well-calibrated (ECE 0.024), top 10% captures ~33% of lead-rich systems. Honest limit: cross-state ROC **0.57** (state-bound).
- **Signature model (methodology track):** leakage-free ROC-AUC **0.683** (the earlier 0.727 had a temporal-leakage flaw and is not used).
- **Strongest spatial signal:** county regional-risk term, +0.047 ROC (regional correlation, not physical cascade).
- **Rejected on evidence:** inter-utility cascade, long-run trajectory layer, log-signature, the replacement-cost "62.7%" (artifact). Depth-3 Stratonovich chosen by paired-CV experiment.
